Product Name: Guess Song

Team Members

Application Overview

Guess song is a game where you have to guess the song playing from the drop down list The song will be a random song from (youtube or spotify) and it will play 10 seconds of the song and then the player will select what they think the song is from a drop down menu

Technology Overview

  • HTML
  • Javascript
  • CSS

Feature List

  • Random song generator from audio hosted on computer
  • Drop down list of song choices
  • button or screen to light up as red for incorrect and green for correct

Future Feature List

    Integrate spotify or youtube for clips of songs
